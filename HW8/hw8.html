Welcome to Domx9200's version of scrabble. This is a one line implementation of it, with score evaluation, shuffling of pieces, resetting the game, and more! This version doesn't include word checking, so any pieces you place will be counted provided the word is starting at the left, and has at least 2 letters. If it isn't valid you won't be able to submit the word.

That being said, here is a rundown of how you play this version of scrabble:
First, you must start your word at the leftmost point
Second, you cannot have a gap in between letters
Third, A word will not be counted or displayed until you place two letters

Note: you won't be able to see your score nor your word in the "Current Word:" or "Word Score:" while you don't have a valid word.
